Why me

Why I actually get this.

Most people who talk have only read about burnout. I actually lived through it. And money, the part most people dread, is the part that always just made sense to me. That combination is the whole point.

  • I burned out chasing grades, then again at my first job. I have properly been through it.
  • Money clicked for me early, mostly from watching, asking, and learning.
  • I explain things like a friend would, simple and straight.

I keep it real with you, just like your most honest friend.

I share what I have learned from my own life, and I do not give financial advice.
